Sound Financial Position

In clearing activities, funds are needed in advance to pay for the consignment, just to mention a few, duties, processing fees, Swiss port/Port charges, Delivery order fees, release fees, etc. All these charges emerge on different stages throughout the clearing process and amounts may vary depending on the nature of consignment.

Therefore, give our clients a freedom to opt for advance payment for duties, shipping line charges and port charges or for specific nature of goods, we do the payment on client’s behalf and be invoiced at the latter stages of the clearing process. This is to enhance efficiency and smoothens of the whole clearing process without any delaying of cargo.

PRE-REQUISITES TO ALL OUT CLIENTS

In order for our company to serve you better we wish to ask all our clients to note the following:

Pakaye Company Limited would like to ask all its clients to provide all their importation / Transit documents as early as possible to avoid delays that may lead to storage and unnecessary inconvenience.

Below is a check list of the entire necessary document.

  1. Original Bill of Lading
  2. Commercial Invoice
  3. Packing list
  4. Exemption Certificate and/or exemption letters if any
  5. T I N Certificate
  6. Identity card (for individual clients)
  7. Authorization letter to Shipping line and TRA advising them that you have authorized our company to clear your consignment on your behalf.
  8. And any other document(s) depending on the nature of the consignment.

We also offer express service to our clients on delayed documents / shipments though, this service is charged different from our regular tariffs upon client’s request. However, we would like to emphasize to all our clients that complete set of documents should be delivered not later than 7 days from the consignment arrival in order to avoid delays that may lead to unnecessary storage charges.
